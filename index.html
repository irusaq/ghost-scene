Spooky Ghost and Graveyard Scene

We created a spooky ghost and graveyard scene because we wanted to experiment with transparent materials, lighting, and spooky animations.

Our Project

Key features of our scene include:

  • Two animated flying ghosts that glide around the graveyard in circular patterns, rising and falling rhythmically
  • Keyboard interactivity:
    • Press 0 to toggle the ghost animation on and off
    • Press 1 to toggle the first row of graves
    • Press 2 to toggle the second row of graves
    • Press 3 to toggle the third row of graves
    • Press 4 to toggle the fourth row of graves
    • Press S to play a spooky sound effect
  • Glowing candles with orange flames and point lights that cast shadows
  • Fences constructed from repeated pickets and a top rail enclosing the graveyard
  • Textured gravestones with curved tops to resemble real headstones
  • Fog and directional lighting to add depth, mood, and shadow effects
