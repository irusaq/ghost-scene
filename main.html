Welcome to our Final Project!

We have created a spooky graveyard scene complete with ghosts, candles, graves, a fence, and fog. To operate: Press 0 to toggle the ghost animation on and off Press 1 to toggle the first row of graves Press 2 to toggle the second row of graves Press 3 to toggle the third row of graves Press 4 to toggle the fourth row of graves Press S to play a spooky sound effect
